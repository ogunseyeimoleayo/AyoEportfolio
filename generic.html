Reflection

In Unit 1, there were details on the different components of Information Security right from the strategic to the operations point of view and how they are all interrelated. Reading through the course lecturecast, I enjoyed acquiring information on the history of ISM. In depth knowlegde of the CIA triad while assessing threats and vulnerabilities. I got to apply the application threat modelling tools STRIDE and DREAD which are used to classify and rate risks respectively. At the end of the unit I had more visibility to different career options in information security

In Unit 2, I had to opportunity to collaborate extensively with my team members on the case study (Compromising a medical mannequin) which gave a lot of insights as to what challenges the medical parastatals face in the rapidly growing cyber world. Compared to other institutions, the medical institutions is highly volatile as cyber attacks can lead to death of patients. There is a need to create more awareness in this space so as to encourage business leaders to invest on next generation application tools to secure their platform and processes not forgetting the need to educate IT personnels on applying the advised industry approved security standards
